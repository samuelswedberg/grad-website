Sim Racing Wheel

A high-performance racing wheel designed from the ground up for sim racing. This project blends mechanical engineering, embedded systems, and motor control.

Key Features

  • Custom-designed aluminum wheel enclosure
  • High-torque direct drive motor with encoder feedback
  • STM32-based motor controller
  • OLED display and RGB LED indicators
  • Dual-clutch paddle system with hall sensors

Tools & Tech

C, FreeRTOS, SPI, UART, CAD in Fusion 360, KiCad for PCB design, 3D printing, and extensive testing with racing simulators.
